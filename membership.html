Membership

Join the Bermuda Volleyball Association

Why Join?

BVA MEMBERSHIP IS YOUR TICKET TO PLAY.

A BVA membership is required to participate in any BVA league, program, or sanctioned event. Membership fees support the development of volleyball across Bermuda — from junior programs to national teams. Youth membership (under 18) is completely free.

Registration

HOW TO
JOIN.

01
Choose Your Plan

Select the membership type that best fits you — Youth (free), Adult, Family, or Associate.

02
Email BVA

Send your name, date of birth, and chosen membership plan to bvamemberships@gmail.com.

03
Complete Payment

BVA will send you payment instructions. Youth memberships require no payment — just registration confirmation.

04
Start Playing

Once confirmed, you're ready to register for leagues, programs, and BVA events throughout the year.
