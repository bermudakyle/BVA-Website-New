The 2025 BVA Summer Beach League concluded with thrilling championship matches at Horseshoe Bay Beach. After 14 weeks of play across the 2v2 and 4v4 divisions, four teams emerged as champions in a final weekend that drew large crowds to the beach.

## Championship Results

**Men's 2v2:** Team Sandstorm (Marcus Powell & Darnell Tucker) def. Team Reef 21–18, 21–15
**Women's 2v2:** Beach Queens (Alicia Darrell & Jasmine Hill) def. Sandy Spikes 21–17, 21–19
**Mixed 4v4 Competitive:** Spike Theory def. Net Gainz 25–22, 21–18, 15–13
**Mixed 4v4 Recreational:** Dig or Die def. Set It and Forget It 21–15, 21–18

## Season Stats

- **Participating teams:** 42
- **Total matches played:** 286
- **Venues:** Horseshoe Bay Beach (Mon/Wed), Elbow Beach (Tue/Thu)
- **Highest attendance weekend:** July 19–20 (approx. 600 spectators)
