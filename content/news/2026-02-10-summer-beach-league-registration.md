The Bermuda Volleyball Association is pleased to announce that registration for the 2026 Summer Beach League is now open. Teams can register for Monday through Thursday play at Horseshoe Bay Beach and Elbow Beach. Both recreational and competitive divisions are available for 2v2 and 4v4 formats.

## League Details

**Season Dates:** May 10 – August 15, 2026
**Locations:** Horseshoe Bay Beach, Elbow Beach
**Formats:** 2v2 and 4v4 (recreational and competitive divisions)
**Play Nights:** Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday

## How to Register

Registration closes **March 1, 2026**. To register your team, contact us at [bermudavolleyball@gmail.com](mailto:bermudavolleyball@gmail.com) or visit the Membership page to complete your registration online.

Teams are encouraged to register early as spots fill up quickly. The summer league is one of BVA's most popular programs, drawing players from across the island each year.

We look forward to seeing you on the sand!
