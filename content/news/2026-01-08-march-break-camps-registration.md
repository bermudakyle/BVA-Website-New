BVA is offering March Break volleyball camps for youth ages 8–17 from March 16–20, 2026. Morning sessions focus on fundamentals and skill-building, while afternoon sessions emphasize competitive play and team development.

## Camp Details

**Dates:** March 16–20, 2026 (Monday–Friday)
**Ages:** 8–17 years old
**Location:** National Sports Centre, Devonshire
**Morning Session:** 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM (Fundamentals & Skills)
**Afternoon Session:** 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM (Competitive Play & Drills)
**Full Day Option:** 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM (includes lunch break)

## Pricing

- Morning Only: $150 per player
- Afternoon Only: $150 per player
- Full Day: $275 per player

## Registration

Spots are limited and fill up fast. To register, visit the [Programs](/programs.html) page or contact us at [bermudavolleyball@gmail.com](mailto:bermudavolleyball@gmail.com).

All skill levels are welcome. Campers should bring their own water bottle, athletic clothing, and volleyball shoes. Equipment will be provided.
