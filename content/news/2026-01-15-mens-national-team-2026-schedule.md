The BVA Men's National Team has released its full 2026 competition calendar. The schedule includes ECVA Championship participation and two international training tours planned for spring and summer.

## 2026 Competition Calendar

**March:** International Training Tour — Trinidad & Tobago
**May:** ECVA Men's Championships (location TBD)
**July:** International Training Tour — Puerto Rico
**August:** NORCECA Continental Championship Qualifier

## Head Coach's Outlook

"2026 is a crucial year for our program," said head coach Marcus James. "The international tours give our players exposure to higher-level competition, and we're building toward NORCECA qualification as a long-term goal."

The national team will hold open tryouts in late February for players interested in joining the program. Watch the BVA website and social channels for tryout date announcements.
