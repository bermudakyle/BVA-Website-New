The BVA Women's National Team has begun an intensive training block ahead of the 2026 Eastern Caribbean Volleyball Association (ECVA) Championships. Head coach Joanne Smith has called up 14 players for the pre-tournament camp starting February 3rd.

## Training Camp Details

The squad will train six days a week at the National Sports Centre through the end of February. The coaching staff has emphasized defensive systems and serve-receive patterns as primary focus areas for this camp cycle.

"We have a talented group that's been working hard all season," said Coach Smith. "The ECVA Championships are a great opportunity for us to compete against top-level Caribbean competition and continue our development."
