Representing Bermuda on the international stage

About the Team

BERMUDA ON THE
WORLD STAGE.

The BVA Men's National Team represents Bermuda in regional and international volleyball competitions. The team competes at the ECVA (Eastern Caribbean Volleyball Association), NORCECA, Island Games, and US Open Championships.

Players selected for the national team are Bermuda's most committed and talented athletes, drawn from BVA's league system. Representation on the national team requires active BVA membership, consistent league participation, and a high level of commitment to training and team responsibilities.

Selection to the Men's National Team is a prestigious honor and represents the highest level of volleyball in Bermuda.
