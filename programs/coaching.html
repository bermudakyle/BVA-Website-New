Coaching Program

Volleyball Canada certified coaching for Bermuda

About the Program

BECOME A
CERTIFIED COACH.

BVA offers coaching certification clinics through Volleyball Canada's National Coaching Certification Program (NCCP). These clinics are typically offered each fall and are open to anyone who wishes to improve their knowledge of the game and become a certified coach.

Whether you want to coach youth volleyball, assist a league team, or develop a national-level program, BVA's coaching certification pathway gives you the skills and credentials to do it right.

BVA strongly encourages all coaches working with junior and national teams to hold at minimum a Level 1 certification.

The CAP is available to coaches who have completed Level 1 and wish to deepen their practical coaching experience. CAP participants work alongside certified coaches in real BVA programs, gaining hands-on training and mentorship.

When Are Clinics Offered?

BVA coaching certification clinics are typically held in the fall season. Announcements are made via BVA's social media channels and email newsletter.

To be notified when the next clinic is scheduled, ensure your BVA membership is active and follow BVA on social media or contact us directly.
