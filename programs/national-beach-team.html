Representing Bermuda in beach volleyball

About the Program

BERMUDA SAND
ON THE WORLD STAGE.

The BVA National Beach Team program develops Bermuda's top beach volleyball players for international competition. The program operates four training groups covering both senior and under-19 levels for men and women.

Beach volleyball is one of the most iconic sports in Bermuda ‚Äî played on the island's famous pink sand beaches and in international arenas. The national beach program connects Bermuda's beach culture with the global game.

Selection to the national beach team requires active BVA membership, consistent participation in the Summer Beach League, and demonstrated skill at the national level.
