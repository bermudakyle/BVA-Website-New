About BVA

Our Mission

Promoting, developing and growing volleyball across Bermuda

Purpose & Values

WHAT WE STAND FOR

The Bermuda Volleyball Association exists to promote the sport of volleyball on our island — from beginner beach players to national team athletes. Our mission is guided by seven core principles.

01
Promote Volleyball
To promote and develop the sport of volleyball in Bermuda at all levels — recreational, competitive, and national — ensuring access for all who wish to play.
02
Develop Athletes
To provide structured pathways for athlete development, from beginner youth camps through junior club programs to national team selection, supported by qualified coaching.
03
Represent Bermuda
To represent Bermuda with excellence in regional and international competition through our Men's National, Women's National, and National Beach Teams.
04
Build Community
To foster a strong and inclusive volleyball community in Bermuda — one that welcomes players of all backgrounds, abilities, and ages to participate and belong.
05
Develop Coaches
To invest in the development of qualified coaches and referees through certification programs that meet international standards and raise the quality of volleyball across the island.
06
Support Youth
To provide opportunities for young Bermudians through youth camps, junior leagues, and financial assistance programs such as bursaries and the Adopt-an-Athlete initiative.
07
Affiliate & Advance
To maintain and strengthen our affiliations with ECVA, NORCECA, and FIVB — participating in the global volleyball community, accessing resources, and advancing the status of Bermuda volleyball internationally.
