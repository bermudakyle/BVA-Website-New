About BVA

Our History

Fifty years of volleyball in Bermuda

Fifty Years Strong

A LEGACY BUILT
ON THE COURT.

Volleyball arrived in Bermuda decades ago and quickly took root as one of the island's most beloved sports. What began as informal beach games and local league play has grown into a structured national program with competitive teams at every level.

The Bermuda Volleyball Association has been the driving force behind this growth — organizing leagues, sending national teams abroad, running youth programs, and building a volleyball community that spans generations.

Today, BVA is a registered charity and proud member of ECVA, NORCECA, and FIVB, representing Bermuda on the world stage.

Milestones

KEY MOMENTS IN
BVA HISTORY

Early 1970s
BVA Founded
The Bermuda Volleyball Association is established, bringing organized volleyball to the island for the first time. Early competitions are held in gymnasiums and on the beach.
1980s
National Teams Formed
BVA establishes both Men's and Women's National Teams, beginning international representation for Bermuda at regional competitions across the Caribbean and North America.
1990s
ECVA & NORCECA Affiliation
BVA becomes an affiliate of the Eastern Caribbean Volleyball Association (ECVA) and NORCECA, opening the door to higher-level regional competition and athlete development opportunities.
2000s
Beach Volleyball Growth
Beach volleyball becomes a major pillar of BVA programming. The Summer Beach League grows rapidly, and Bermuda begins fielding national beach teams for international competition.
2010s
Junior Programs Launch
BVA launches structured junior development programs — Paradise Hitters (Girls) and Big Wave Riders (Boys) — creating a clear pathway from youth volleyball to the national team.
2015
Island Games
Bermuda competes in volleyball at the NatWest Island Games, showcasing the island's talent on the multi-sport international stage alongside other island territories.
2022
Silver at Nike Festival
The Women's National Team earns a silver medal at the prestigious Nike International Volleyball Festival — one of BVA's most celebrated achievements to date.
2024
50th Anniversary
BVA celebrates over 50 years of volleyball in Bermuda. The anniversary is marked with special events, recognition of long-serving members, and a renewed commitment to growing the sport.
