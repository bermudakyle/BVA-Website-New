Adopt-an-Athlete

Sponsor a Bermuda volleyball athlete competing internationally

Make a Difference

FUEL BERMUDA'S
VOLLEYBALL ATHLETES.

Representing Bermuda abroad is a costly endeavour. Travel, accommodation, competition fees and equipment add up quickly. The Adopt-an-Athlete program allows the community to directly sponsor athletes competing at ECVA, NORCECA, Island Games, and US Open level events.

Process

HOW IT WORKS

01
Choose a Tier
Select the donation tier that works for you — from $50 Fan to $500 Champion level sponsorship.
02
Contact BVA
Email bermudavolleyball@gmail.com with your preferred tier and any specific athlete or team you wish to support.
03
Make Your Donation
BVA will provide payment instructions. All donations go directly toward athlete travel and competition costs.
04
Follow the Journey
Receive updates on your athlete's competition results and be recognized in BVA communications.
