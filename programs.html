Our Programs

FIND YOUR
PROGRAM.

Junior Girls · Ages 12–18
Paradise Hitters
Girls competitive volleyball program. Year-round training 2–3x/week with league play and international competition pathways.
Junior Boys · Ages 12–18
Big Wave Riders
Boys competitive volleyball program developing Bermuda's next generation of champions through structured training and competition.
National Team
Men's National Team
Representing Bermuda in ECVA, NORCECA, Island Games and US Open Championships.
National Team
Women's National Team
Silver medallists at the Nike International Volleyball Festival. Competing at ECVA and NORCECA level.
Camps · Ages 8–14
Youth Camps
March Break & Summer volleyball camps. Fun, skills, and great volleyball for young athletes.
National Team
National Beach Team
Senior and U19 beach programs representing Bermuda at ECVA and NORCECA international competitions.
Development
Coaching Program
Volleyball Canada Level 1 & Level 2 coaching certifications offered each fall for aspiring coaches.
