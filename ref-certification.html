Referee Certification

Become a certified volleyball referee in Bermuda

Referee Program

OFFICIATE THE
GAME YOU LOVE.

BVA runs referee certification clinics to develop qualified officials for Bermuda's volleyball leagues and national competitions. Certified referees are essential to the integrity of the game, and BVA is always looking for new officials to join our referee pool.

Whether you're a retired player, a volleyball parent, or someone who simply loves the sport, becoming a certified referee is a great way to give back to the Bermuda volleyball community.

Referee clinics are held periodically throughout the year. Check BVA's social media for upcoming dates or contact us to register your interest.
