Annual Reports

Transparency and accountability for our volleyball community

Accountability

BVA ANNUAL
REPORTS

As a registered charity (Charity #646), the Bermuda Volleyball Association is committed to transparency and accountability to our members and the wider community. Annual reports are published following each AGM and are available to members upon request.

Reports include the year's financial summary, program highlights, competition results, and goals for the coming season.

Annual General Meeting

BVA holds an Annual General Meeting (AGM) each year. All members are eligible to attend. Meeting notices are distributed via email and social media.

If you are a BVA member and wish to receive AGM notifications, ensure your membership details are up to date: bvamemberships@gmail.com
