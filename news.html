Summer 2026

Summer Beach League Registration Now Open

The Bermuda Volleyball Association is pleased to announce that registration for the 2026 Summer Beach League is now open. Teams can register for Monday through Thursday play at Horseshoe Bay Beach and Elbow Beach. Both recreational and competitive divisions are available for 2v2 and 4v4 formats.

National Teams
Women's National Team Prepares for 2026 ECVA Championships
January 28, 2026

The BVA Women's National Team has begun intensive preparation for the upcoming ECVA Championships. Head Coach Gary LeBlanc has been working with the squad through a rigorous training program aimed at building on last year's silver medal performance at the Nike International Volleyball Festival.

National Teams
Men's National Team Announces 2026 Competition Schedule
January 15, 2026

The BVA Men's National Team has confirmed their 2026 competition calendar including NORCECA qualifying events, the Island Games, and the US Open Championships. Tryouts for new squad members will be announced shortly.

Youth Programs
March Break Volleyball Camps — Registration Open
January 8, 2026

BVA Youth Volleyball Camps are back for March Break 2026! Two weeks of skills-based training at Warwick Academy Gymnasium. Spaces are limited — register early to secure your spot. Ages 8–14, $300/week.

Leagues
2025 Summer League Wrap-Up: Champions Crowned at Horseshoe Bay
October 3, 2025

The 2025 BVA Summer Beach League concluded with an exciting championship weekend at Horseshoe Bay. This season saw record participation across both recreational and competitive divisions, with over 40 teams competing throughout the summer.

Tournaments
Bermuda Open 2025 Recap: International Teams Shine
August 18, 2025

The annual Bermuda Open drew teams from across the Caribbean and North America to Horseshoe Bay Beach. The 4v4 format delivered spectacular beach volleyball action over two exciting days of competition.

Junior Programs
Paradise Hitters and Big Wave Riders Complete Fall Season
November 22, 2025

Both BVA junior programs had a strong fall season with multiple players showing significant development. Several athletes from the Paradise Hitters program have been identified for potential national team consideration.
