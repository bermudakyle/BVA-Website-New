BVA Bursary

Financial assistance for Bermudian student athletes

About the Bursary

VOLLEYBALL SHOULD BE ACCESSIBLE TO ALL.

The Bermuda Volleyball Association believes that financial barriers should never prevent a talented and committed athlete from participating in volleyball. The BVA Bursary Program provides financial assistance to help student athletes with the costs associated with BVA programs and memberships.

The bursary is awarded as a $500 partial scholarship applied toward the first term of program fees. Applications are reviewed by the BVA executive committee and awarded based on financial need and commitment to the sport.

Application Deadline
December 12, 2025
$500
Partial scholarship toward first term program fees
Eligibility Requirements
  • ✓ Bermudian citizen or permanent resident
  • ✓ Currently enrolled as a student
  • ✓ Active BVA member or applicant
  • ✓ Demonstrated financial need
  • ✓ Commitment to BVA program participation
Application

HOW TO
APPLY.

01
Review Eligibility

Confirm you meet the eligibility requirements. The bursary is open to Bermudian students with demonstrated financial need.

02
Complete the Form

Fill out the BVA Bursary application form online. The form asks about your background, volleyball involvement, and financial circumstances.

03
Submit by Deadline

Submit your application before December 12, 2025. Late applications will not be considered for the current bursary cycle.

04
Await Decision

The BVA executive committee will review all applications and notify successful applicants. Results are communicated by email.

Apply for the Bursary
Application deadline: December 12, 2025
