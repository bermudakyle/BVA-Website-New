Official Rules

BVA LEAGUE
RULES OVERVIEW

BVA league play follows the official FIVB (Fédération Internationale de Volleyball) rules of the game as the foundation, with BVA-specific amendments to accommodate local conditions, venue limitations, and the recreational/competitive balance of Bermuda league play.

The complete amended rules document is available for download below. All team captains and coaches are expected to be familiar with the current rules before the season begins.

Key Rules at a Glance

Indoor 6v6
  • — Rally point scoring; matches best of 3 or 5 sets (format determined per league)
  • — Sets played to 25 points; deciding set to 15 points (minimum 2-point lead)
  • — Libero position permitted per FIVB rules
  • — Rosters: minimum 6 players to start, up to 12 rostered players
  • — Substitutions: up to 6 per set per team
  • — Active BVA membership required for all players
Beach & Grass Leagues
  • — Beach: 2v2 and 4v4 formats; sets to 21 (best of 3) or 15 (deciding)
  • — Grass: 6v6 outdoor format following indoor rules with surface-appropriate modifications
  • — No Libero in beach or grass formats
  • — Beach: hand setting restrictions apply per FIVB beach rules
  • — Rally point scoring throughout
  • — Active BVA membership required for all players
General BVA Rules
  • — All registered players must have a valid BVA membership for the current season
  • — Teams are responsible for ensuring all their players are registered before play
  • — BVA has the right to forfeit results where unregistered players competed
  • — Unsportsmanlike conduct may result in suspension from BVA leagues
  • — Schedule conflicts must be reported to BVA at least 48 hours in advance
  • — Final authority on rule interpretation rests with BVA
